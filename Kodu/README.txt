This Kodu interpreter was created by Troi A. Williams in 2013 as part
of his MS thesis research at Norfolk State University.  Cite:

  Williams, T. A. (2014) Programming idealized robots in
  the harsh real world. MS thesis, Computer Science Department,
  Norfolk State University, Norfolk, VA.

Additional features for multi-robot interaction were added in 2015 by
Matt Dee, Alex Franchuk, Ashwath Sankar, Vikram Sunder, Brandon
Tolsch, and Dave Touretzky.
